The ***Pizza Time Theatre 1980 Spot*** is an unnamed commercial that introduces the Pizza Time Players, food, tokens, and games! The commercial was filmed some time before May of 1980 and would be used until 1981.{{< cite 2 >}}

### History
Filming took place over two days at two locations with the oversight of Bob Wilkins Advertising Inc.{{< cite 1 >}} The footage from {{< wiki-link "Concord, CA (1975 Diamond Blvd)" >}} would be of the gameroom and guests, while {{< wiki-link "Citrus Heights, CA (7614 Greenback Lane)" >}} had footage of its {{< wiki-link "Cyberamics" >}}. The only currently known airing of the commercial has {{< wiki-link "Rockville, MD (855 Rockville Pike)" >}} mentioned in its outro.
