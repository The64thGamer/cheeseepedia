The ***Pizza Time Theatre 1980 Spot*** is an unnamed commercial that introduces the Pizza Time Players, food, tokens, and games! The commercial was filmed some time before May of 1980 and would be used until 1981.[2]

### History
Filming took place over two days at two locations with the oversight of Bob Wilkins Advertising Inc.[1] The footage from [Concord, CA (1975 Diamond Blvd)] would be of the gameroom and guests, while [Citrus Heights, CA (7614 Greenback Lane)] had footage of its [Cyberamics]. The only currently known airing of the commercial has [Rockville, MD (855 Rockville Pike)] mentioned in its outro.
