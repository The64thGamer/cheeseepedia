***Limbo Rock*** was the main live show for the {{< wiki-link "September 2000 Show" >}}. It is a cover of ['Limbo Rock'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Limbo_Rock). It later returned in April 2004 on the {{< wiki-link "Road Show 2004" >}} CD which played until December 31, 2004.

## Summary
The live show consists of Chuck E. and the band singing ‘Limbo Rock'. The footage is of the band at the beach, which is reused from {{< wiki-link "September '94" >}}.
