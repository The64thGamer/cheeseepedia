The Billy Bob Animatronic was one of the main [Classic Stage] animatronics produced by Creative Engineering Inc. The animatronic would first be developed in April of 1980, before debuting at the [Jacksonville, FL (9820 Atlantic Blvd)] on August 14th of that year.

## History

Billy Bob was first designed by Creative Engineering's main artist Dave Thomas at the request of [Aaron Fechter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aaron_Fechter) and was named after two people, David ,,Billy Bob'' Irvin,one of the artists at [Creative Engineering] and Aaron's college roommate, and Robert L. Brock (Bob Brock)the co-founder of [Showbiz Pizza Place] (2) .In the original Concept art (1) Billy Bob is significantly thinner with lighter colored fur similar to some of the later versions of the [Billy Wilbur (Animatronic)] .

The first Billy Bob animatronic debuted on august 14th 1981 at the [Jacksonville, FL (9820 Atlantic Blvd)] store.This version of Billy Bob had very dark brown fur,Claws painted brown and shared his mask with the second iteration of the character [Billy Wilbur (Animatronic)] dubbed as the ,,Beta'' mask (3).This design would be slightly toned down with the next few bots produced,giving Billy Bob shorter but still dark fur.In 1981 after months of work, the art department headed by Essie Fechter,with the artists led by Dave Thomas, the newly designed Billy Bob was brought upstairs from the art department of [Creative Engineering] which resided in the basement of the [Orlando, FL (47 W Jefferson St)] plant.This new redesign of Billy Bob gave him significantly lighter fur. (4) Shortly after this Billy Bob's mask was resculpted again,typically dubbed as the ,,Showbiz Standandard mask''

 Billy Bob animatronics would continue to be produced up until 1983. The January batch would be the last used inside ShowBiz Pizza restaurants, and would be kept and stored for future openings, while Creative Engineering would continue to produce bots until June. Now with a surplus of bots, these would need to be sold to other family entertainment centers. Through the [Concept Unification] process, Billy Bob would be the only character not used during this process, and was ordered to be destroyed or used for parts.(5)

Around 1995 Billy Bob's mask was resculpted again for unknown reasons,but it's likley due to [Creative Engineering] at one point losing the original mold for his mask.This version was used at various venues such as [Animal House Pizza] , [Gulliver's Kingdom Stage] , [Gulliver's World Stage] , [Gulliver's Land Stage].Shortly after another mask was sculpted for Billy Bob off of a plug of the Showbiz Standard mask to give him more round and defined features,this mask is still in use to this day at varios venues and is considered the ,,current'' mask version.

Alongside this mask another version of the mask dubbed as ,,Crowsfoot'' sculpted in 1981 was accidentally used on a few shows notably [Adventure Landing] , and the first mask on the [Tweetsie Railroad Stage] .This mask was very porly sculpted,with the mouth being far too shallow and the creases on the side of the face are practically non-existent.This caused the mask to be put under intense stress causing it to rip and deform very quickly.In 2024 [Creative Engineering] as an experiment went back in and added improved mouth creases similar in style to those used on the Showbiz standard Billy Bob mask (6)

## Character

Billy Bob was the primary mascot of [ShowBiz Pizza Place].He's a lovable hillbilly bear who works at Smitty's Super Service Station along with his pal [Looney Bird (Animatronic)].Billy Bob is voiced by [Aaron Fechter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aaron_Fechter) who initially gave the character a very high pitched voice that would perpetually be toned down in later showtapes.Billy Bob and Looney in the early segments most notably [Goofy Gas Commercial] created a fuel alternative called Goofy Gas that could both be used as fule for a car and as an alcoholic beverage.This aspect would slowly be dropped over the years.

When the first Showbiz Pizza Place oppened in [Kansas City (Antioch), MO (2726 NE Vivion Rd)] despite Billy Bob being used all over the marketing of the store along with the [Wolf Pack 5] he did not apear in animatronic form but rather as the [Billy Bob Kansas City Walkaround]

## Design

The bot is a brown bear in yellow and red striped overalls, holding a 4 string guitar carved out of a log with bones as tuning knobs. He has two ears, beige skin with rosy red cheeks,one tooth sticking out of his bottom jaw,dark blue eyes and black claws. A yellow bird with white and green tufts on it's head nicknamed 'Birthday Bird' sits on the neck of his guitar near the head.

## Mechanical Structure

The frame of Billy Bob's mech is mainly constructed out of alluminum square bars(hollow tube for the beta bots,solid for standard production) with various smaller steal parts,includig rods,clevises and mounts.The character like almost every other [Creative Engineering] bot runs on pneumatic cylinders produced by the companies **Cylinders & valves inc.** , **Fabco-Air Inc** and **Clippard Minimatic**.These cylinders are than actuated with **Mac valves**
