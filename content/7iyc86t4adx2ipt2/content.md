## Trivia

- A different device called the Tune Machine [Madame Oink IV - The Tune Machine]. This show is referenced as Chuck E. refers to the Tune Machine as being "part time machine" and "part jukebox", which is how Madame Oink describes the Tune Machine in Segment 1 of the original show. 
- When the band arrives in Camelot in Segment 2, the early 80s trumpet cheer plays.
