The ***Black Pizza Time Theatre Exterior Sign*** was an exterior sign installed on most [Pizza Time Theatre] stores from May/June 1979 to early 1981. The Exterior Sign was first used at the Pizza Time Theatre on [Concord, CA (1975 Diamond Blvd)] and last removed at the closure of [Ogden, UT (3155 Harrison Blvd)] in 1991.

## History

The Black PTT Exterior Sign was created as the mostly standard PTT exterior logo as both [San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd)] and [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)] used non standard Exterior Signs. The Black PTT Exterior Sign was used on most PTT exteriors from 1979 to early 1981, however, some used plain Yellow letter signage.[1] The sign was replaced on most PTT stores during the early 1980's due to most getting the [PTT 1981 Exterior Update]. The last sign was removed after he closure of [Ogden, UT (3155 Harrison Blvd)] in 1991.

## Design

The sign was the 1979 to 1981 Black & Yellow Pizza Time Theatre Logo.
