The ***Pizza Time Theatre Show Selector*** is a prototype selection unit known to be at the [San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd)] and possibly at other early stores, most likely with [Huntington Beach, CA (15511 Edwards St)]. The units would be set up with Cabaret or Lounge acts and were coin operated.

## History

Shortly after [Kooser Dolli Dimples (Animatronic)] was installed at [San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd)], Pizza Time Theatre prototyped for an alteration to Dolli's show playback to have a jukebox unit that could have coins inserted into it to play specific shows. [Huntington Beach, CA (15511 Edwards St)] possibly had one installed with their [Helen Henny (Lounge)] either at or near opening, as it is noted both stores had their acts be coin-operated. PTT would perform a survey for their coin boxes of their various locations, and noticed both acts had a 1:2 quarter to token ratio, unlike the usual arcades which had a 1:6 ratio. PTT believed this showed adults were activating the shows more than kids.

The Kooser prototype box would be moved throughout the early corporate offices before eventually being thrown in a scrap pile at the [Sunnyvale, CA (1213 Innsbruck Drive)]. Dale Benjamin would recover the box and keep it with them to present day.[1]
