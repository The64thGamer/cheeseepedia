***YMCA 2003*** was the main live show for the [Chuck E. Cheese Spring Show 2003], which was also used with [Summertime 2003]. It is a cover of ['Y.M.C.A.'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Y.M.C.A._(song)) with Chuck E. singing lead. 
## Summary
The live show consists of Chuck E. singing ‘YMCA' with Jasper and Pasqually singing backup. The footage is animated text saying ‘Chuck E. Live’ and ‘YMCA’. The birthday break is animated text that says ‘CEC TV’.
## Live Show List:
1. ['Y.M.C.A.'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Y.M.C.A._(song))
2. Birthday Break
3. [New Birthday (2002)]
