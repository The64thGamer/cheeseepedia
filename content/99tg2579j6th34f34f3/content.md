***Campbell, CA (30 S. Central Ave)*** was the first [Zapp's Bar & Grill], going under ***Gilbert Zapp's World Tour***, then ***Gilbert Zapp's***, then ***Zapp's***, from September of 1982 to the early 90's.[2][7][6] Notably this location featured no animatronics.[2]


## History

### Before Pizza Time Theatre
The location would originally open as ***Stan Becker's The Prune Pit*** in January of 1970, being a bar hosting popular live shows of rock and blues. After construction issues the bar would reponen in August of 1970 as ***The Bodega***.[3][2][4] On the last night of the bar before its closing sometime in September, Tiran Porter would be playing.[2]

### Gilbert Zapp's
The location would reopen shortly after its closure in September of 1982. The location would keep its previous manager, Danna, through the transition to Pizza Time Theatre's ownership.[6] One of the earliest performances at the reopened location would be "A.J And the Shapes" [2]
