The ***94-95 Rock-afire Explosion Demo Tape*** was a promotional video tape given to potential buyers of Creative Engineering shows in the mid to late 90's, created in 1994.

## Summary

The tape consists of Billy Bob and Fatz Geronimo going over the entirety of the [New Rock-afire Explosion], its features, and its variations. Many segments let full songs from showtapes play out. The latter half also goes over the original [Rock-afire Explosion] show, now called the 'Classic' Rock-afire.
