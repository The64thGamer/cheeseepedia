***Sterling Heights, MI (13745 Lakeside Circle) #103*** originally opened up on February 2, 2000, as a Chuck E. Cheese's [1] and is still open today as a Chuck E. Cheese.

## History
### 2000's
The Sterling Heights, MI, Chuck E. Cheese's opened on February 2, 2000. It was one of 10 locations CEC Entertainment acquired from Discovery Zone to become a Chuck E. Cheese resturant'[2]. The store featured an [Studio C Alpha], New Games, Decor from the [SPT 1990's Remodel Program], and the newest features from [Phase 3 (2000)]. Between 2002 and 2008, the Chuck E. Cheese animatronic would obtain the Cool Chuck shirt and shorts. [3] Between 2008 and 2012, the Chuck E. Cheese animatronic would obtain the Avenger shirt, shorts, and hat[4].

### 2010's
In late 2012 Sterling Heights would undergo the [CEC 2000's Remodel Program] during the remodel the [Interactive Console] was removed being replaced with the "CEC" panel, and the Blue Screen camera was removed and replaced with the [Ticket Blaster].[5]In 2016 the store recieved [Chuck E.'s Play Pass].[12]In 2017 the Blue Screen would be removed [7], the SkyTubes would also be removed that year [8]. In 2018, the Chuck E. Cheese animatronic would receive the Rockstar Shirt. Between late 2017 and early 2018, the curtains on the [Studio C Alpha] would be disabled. [6]  

### 2020's
March 2020 would mark the store's temporary closing due to COVID-19. On October 9, 2020, the store would re-open for "Games & Dine-In".[9] On August 13, 2023, Sterling Heights would begin its [CEC 2.0 Remodel Program] after minor construction delays regarding the POD Trailers.[10] The stage would be removed that night alongside the artwork. The store would gain the 2019 model [Chuck E. Live Stage (Dance Floor)], with the [Video Wall] being installed on the back wall, with the former stage being replaced with booths. On October 5, 2023, the store would celebrate its Grand Re-Opening with Helen[11]. In 2024, the store would receive the Chuck E. Cheese Trampoline Zone and Trampoline Zone artwork of Bella B., Chuck E. Cheese, and Mr. Munch[13].
