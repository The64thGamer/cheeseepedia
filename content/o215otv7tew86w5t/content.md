Beach Bear was created for the [Wolf Pack 5] show and debuted at the 1978 IAAPA. His mask uses the same mold as [Boom Boom Bosco (Animatronic)] from the [Bear Country Jubilee].

## Design

Beach Bear is a gray polar bear that plays an electric guitar. He wore a lei and swim trunks with varying colors depending on location.

## Movements

- Mouth
- Head left
- Head right
- Head up
- Left eyelid raise
- Right eyelid raise
- Eye cross
- Strum
- Fret slide
- Guitar raise
- Foot tap
