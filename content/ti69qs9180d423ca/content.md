The ***Winchester Flag Wavers* was an animatronic produced by [Fantasy Forest] for the [Pizza Time Theatre] on [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)] in May 1977. The Bot was a collection of waving flags that were around the room that were controlled through one animatronic system.
The Bot was used from May 1977 up until either Winchester got [Cyberamics] in 1981, or when the location closed in 1985.**

## History

The Winchester Flag Wavers were installed in May 1977 in the dining areahttps://www.showbizpizza.com/info/promo/ptt/ptt_79franchise.pdf (Contains the Thumbnail image & Broad Dining Area Shot.). They would wave along to the showtapes[1]. The Bot appeared in the 'The Great All-American Pizza Show' Brochure in 1979, where there referred to as just 'flags'[1]. The Bot would remain at Winchester until it got Cyberamics in 1981. The Bot's were either sent to [Sunnyvale, CA (1213 Innsbruck Drive)], or thrown out.
