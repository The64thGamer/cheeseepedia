***6301 Richmond Hwy, Store #75*** is a *Chuck E. Cheese's* that opened on November 20, 2010 with a [Studio C Beta] and is still open today with the 2.0 remodel. This store is the relocation of [Alexandria, VA (6303 Richmond Hwy)], which opened on September 20, 1982 with a [Cyberamics Balcony Stage] and closed between November and December 2009 with a [2-Stage].

## Stages

Originally when they opened, they had a Studio C Beta. They would have the stage until July 2023, when it was removed for a Dance Floor and Video Wall.

## History

Alexandria opened on November 20, 2010 with the [CEC 2000's Remodel Program] and a [Studio C Beta]. Not much info is known about Alexandria in general due to lack of documentation. In July 2023, the store got 2.0, removing the Beta for a Dance Floor and Video Wall. As of August 2023, Alexandria is still open with 2.0.
