The ***Mini Billy Bob Toy Fair Promo* showtape was a tape used exclusively at the New York Toy Fair from February 10th-19th, 1986.
The tape runs on a unique stage setup, featuring a standard [Billy Bob (Animatronic)] and eight of the [Mini Billy Bob (Animatronic)]. It is unknown if each mini Billy Bob were programmed independently in sync, or if they were all running off the same data lines.**
