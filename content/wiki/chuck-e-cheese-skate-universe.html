Gameplay

Chuck E. Cheese's Skate Universe is an endless runner video game. The game starts by Clicking On the Skate Button On The park Select Screen, Chuck E. Will Begin The Marathon While Skating, the player can swipe up, down, left, or right to avoid crashing into oncoming obstacles Such as Blocks. By swiping rapidly as speed increases, more points can be acquired. A crash results in a game over. The player can collect 2 currencies, Fish And Pizza. Fish Can Be Found In Yeti River Run and Sweet Scape, And While Ony Pizza Can Be Collected On Coconut Park.
