***Salisbury, MD (2300 N Salisbury Blvd)*** is a Chuck E. Cheese's that opened on August 6, 2004, located at The Centre At Salisbury, and is set to close on April 4, 2026.

## History

### Pre-opening

On January 5, 2004, CEC Entertainment confirmed that a new location in Salisbury would open in The Centre at Salisbury for a planned October opening. The next day, an article was published stating 'Chuck E. Cheese's to open in fall'.{{< cite 1 >}} Starting in March 2004, CEC Entertainment would begin hiring managers.{{< cite 2 >}}In April 2004, CEC Entertainment would apply for the 'Class 'B' Beer and Wine (On/Off Sale)(7 Day) Restaurant Alcohol Beverage License'; the hearing would take place on April 20, 2004{{< cite 3 >}} Beginning in June 2004 The Center At Salisbury would begin mentioning the new Chuck E. Cheese's in marketing{{< cite 4 >}}On Wednesday August 4, 2004 the Chuck E. Cheese's in Salisbury would host a 'pre-opening party' before it's official grand opening on August 6, 2004.{{< cite 5 >}}

### 2000's

On August 6, 2004, the Chuck E. Cheese's in Salisbury would officially open to the public. {{< cite 6 >}}

### 2010's

During the 2010's, the Chuck E. Cheese's in Salisbury would repaint some walls purple and receive the Ticket Blaster. During Summer 2017, the store would receive the{{< wiki-link "Chuck E.'s Play Pass" >}}{{< cite 7 >}}

### 2020's

In March 2020, the location would close due to COVID-19. On July 15, 2020, the location would re-open{{< cite 8 >}}. Later that year, the store would remove the {{< wiki-link "Skytubes" >}} and {{< wiki-link "Ticket Muncher" >}} as apart of the {{< wiki-link "2020 COVID-19 Removals" >}}. In 2024, the location would receive the {{< wiki-link "Trampoline Zone" >}}{{< cite 9 >}}. Beginning in April 2024, begin participation in the {{< wiki-link "Low Volume Store Initiative" >}}{{< cite 11 >}}. On March 9, 2026, it was announced that the Salisbury Chuck E. Cheese's would cease operations on April 6, 2026. {{< cite 10 >}}
