The ***Pizza Time Theatre 1980 Spot*** is an unnamed commercial that introduces the Pizza Time Players, food, tokens, and games! The commercial would be used until 1981{{< cite 2 >}}

## Production 

Bob Wilkins Advertising Inc produced the commercial; filming took place over two days.{{< cite 1 >}}
Narrator - Unknown
