***Whac-A-Munch*** was a modified version of the original Whac-A-Mole cabinet exclusive to Chuck E. Cheese's from an unknown set of years, presumed around the early 90's with one cabinet showing a January 1992 construction date.


### Variants

Each cabinet seen of Whac-A-Munch features multiple different color schemes and styles.
