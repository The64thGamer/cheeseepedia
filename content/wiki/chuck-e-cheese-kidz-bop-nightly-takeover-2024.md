***'Chuck E. Cheese KIDZ BOP Nightly Takeover 2024'*** was the night summer show released July 8, 2024 and played until September 9, 2024.
This showtape was created to promote the KIDZ BOP Nightly Takeover event at Chuck E. Cheese. This showtape features 0 programmed CEC songs. This show ran from 6pm to closing, the standard showtape would play outside the timeframe.
