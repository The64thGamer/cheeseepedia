Cheese's Photo Ride (1993) is a arcade game of chuck e cheese with the ptt chuck e design created in 1993 by Amutec, this is the first photo ride of chuck e cheese and was very rare because the photo ride was only in some locations,sadly there is no more 1993 photo rides in stores.

Gameplay you need to insert a token into the token slot o tap the play pass to play the ride is going to play a audio track also the car is going to doing a rocking motion, in the car they are 5 characters buttons you can push the buttons to play characters quotes
