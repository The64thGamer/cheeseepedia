## Trivia:

- A majority of the Existing Stages version of this show is intermission-based, with only around 15 minutes of programmed material in total.
- The songs 'Rock Around The Clock', Donald O'Connor - 'Make 'Em Laugh', 'Camptown Races', 'Oh Susanna', 'She'll Be Coming Around The Mountain', 'She'll Be Making a Combo Pizza', and 'Mack The Knife' all have unique footage created for it.
- This was the last show produced in the 'talk show' format. 
- Charlie Rockit is listed as a cast member in the 'International Mouse of Mystery' skit, but does not appear in this show.
