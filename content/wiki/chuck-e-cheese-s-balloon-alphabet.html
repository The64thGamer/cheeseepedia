Cheese’s Balloon Alphabet was a game developed by UFO interactive games. It was released on September 20th, 2011, and was available for purchase on the App Store for ¢99. The official description of the game read:
“GUESS THE LETTERS! Pop the balloons and guess the correct letters to the identify the picture shown! Pop quickly to get the highest score! A brand new puzzle game from UFO Interactive Games and Chuck E. Cheese's! Multiple backgrounds to choose from! Fun for the whole family! – Simple gameplay mechanics with a low learning curve that mimic the games at the retail Chuck E. Cheese's locations will have your family playing in no time! Fun For Everyone - Providing wholesome, enjoyable family entertainment that makes the Chuck E. Cheese's experience magical! Don't forget to check out our other Chuck E. Cheese's Games! Chuck E. Cheese's was founded by Nolan Bushnell (founder of Atari & Pong), in May 1977 and has finally come back full circle to be released on the new generation of gaming platforms! www.ufoplay.com - Visit our website for more news and info about our games!”
