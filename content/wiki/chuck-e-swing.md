***'Chuck E. Swing'*** was a generic live show which was released alongside {{< wiki-link "April 1999" >}} and could be played until the end of June 2008. 

## Summary

The footage is a parody of the GAP 'Khaki Swing' commercial, with a CEC version of the GAP logo appearing at the end. The birthday break music is a piano version of 'Chuck E. Swing'.

## Live Show List:

1. 'Chuck E. Swing'
2. Birthday Break
3. {{< wiki-link "Birthday Star '98" >}}
