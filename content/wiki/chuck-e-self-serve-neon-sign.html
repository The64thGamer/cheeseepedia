Cheese's Self-Serve Neon Sign was a fixture found at Chuck E. Cheese's Pizza and ShowBiz Pizza locations installed during the 1986 ShowBiz Pizza Time remodel program.

Design

The design features Chuck holding a Coca-Cola cup with a speech bubble saying 'Self Serve'. It has various colors such as green, red, white and blue.
