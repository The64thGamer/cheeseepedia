***Makin’ It*** was the main live show for the {{< wiki-link "September 2003 Show" >}}. It is a cover of ['Makin’ It.'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Makin%27_It_(song)) with Chuck E. singing lead. 

## Summary
The live show consists of Chuck E. singing ‘Makin’ It' with the rest of the band singing backup. The footage is animated text saying ‘Chuck E. Live’ with a swirl background.
