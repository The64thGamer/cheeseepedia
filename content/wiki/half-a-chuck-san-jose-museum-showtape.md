The ***Half-A-Chuck San Jose Museum Showtape*** is a showtape released for the {{< wiki-link "Half-A-Chuck (Cyberamic)" >}} at the Foothill College Electronics Museum in San Jose, California. The showtape would be played from the character's installation in 1992 until the museum's closure in 1991, and consists of a single skit repeated 24 times.
