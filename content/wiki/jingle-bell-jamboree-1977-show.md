The ***Jingle Bell Jamboree 1977 Show*** was a CEI 8-Track Reel Showtape used for Santa's Village's {{< wiki-link "Jingle Bell Jamboree" >}} by Creative Engineering. The Showtape was used from the Show's installation to 1979 in which it was replaced with the {{< wiki-link Jingle Bell Jamboree 1979 Show >}}.
