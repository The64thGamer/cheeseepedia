Cheese International Show 2 2023 is the Spring show released on April 17, 2023 featuring a variety of songs released from 2016-2019. This show is intended for all international locations to play. (Excluding Canada, Puerto Rico, and Mexico) Mexico will play the {{< wiki-link "Chuck E Cheese Kidz Bop Show 2023" >}} while Canada and Puerto Rico play the {{< wiki-link "Chuck E Cheese Spring Show 2023" >}} The automatic live is Spring Break Break Dance.
