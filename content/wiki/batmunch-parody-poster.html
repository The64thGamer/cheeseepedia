Cheese's"] +++ BatMunch was a poster created for Showbiz Pizza and Chuck e cheese probably in 1989 or 1990.

History

This poster was one of the many Chuck E Cheese's parody posters released in 1988. It is believed that after 1991, production of this poster and the other Chuck E Cheese's movie parody posters would cease. Over the years, this poster would become very rare to find in stores nationwide as remodels would occur and the removal of this poster and rest of the movie parody posters.

Design

The poster is a parody of the 1989 film Batman with Munch as Batman. In the top right of the poster there's a quote that reads "Starring Mister munch as "BatMunch" Co.starring Vera Stout and Bustin Seams And Featuring Railey O'Beese as "Major Luvhandels"
