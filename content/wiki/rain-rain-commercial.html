Summary

The commercial begins with Chuck E. sitting outside of a home with 3 children while its raining, Chuck E. raises his hand then the rain quickly clears up. It then cuts to the three children playing and eating at a decorated Chuck E. Cheese's. Before the commercial ends a second rain cloud appears over one of the children, but Chuck E. swats the cloud away before it could rain.
