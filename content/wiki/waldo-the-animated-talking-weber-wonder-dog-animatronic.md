The Waldo animatronic was an animatronic produced for **Weber** by **Creative Presentations Inc** *sometime* *around 1978* to be used in promotional shows advertising Weber Grills.

## Movements<sup>(1)</sup>

- Head
- Nose
- Eyes
- Ears
- Tail
- Mouth
