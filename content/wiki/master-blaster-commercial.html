Summary

This commercial follows the guests at Chuck E. Cheese's playing games alongside Chuck E. Cheese. Above them would be words like Winning, Soarer, and Grinner. The ending of the commercial sees Chuck E. sitting in front of a collage of photos of children playing the games.

Production

Zambrelli used USanimation / ToonBoom to animate the commercial. It is currently unknown which location was used for filming.
