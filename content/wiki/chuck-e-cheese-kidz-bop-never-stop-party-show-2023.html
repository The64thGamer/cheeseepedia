Cheese KIDZ BOP Never Stop Party Show 2023 is the night summer show released July 26, 2023. This showtape was created to promote the KIDZ BOP Never Stop Party series of concerts. This showtape features 4 programmed CEC songs from 2017 - 2023 and two new DJ Munch Remixes which are not programmed. This show runs from 6pm to closing, the {{< wiki-link "Chuck E Cheese Summer Show 2023" >}} will play outside the timeframe for this show. This is the second CEC Media Network exclusive show.
