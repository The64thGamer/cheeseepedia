The ***Peter Piper Pizza 2.0 Remodel Program*** is a remodel initiative for {{< wiki-link "Peter Piper Pizza" >}} locations that started in April of 2013 with test locations and a logo redesign, before the company would be aquired by {{< wiki-link "Chuck E. Cheese's" >}} in 2014 and the final remodel being completed and pushed nationwide in 2016 around the same time as the {{< wiki-link "CEC 2.0 Remodel Program" >}}.{{< cite 1 >}}{{< cite 2 >}}{{< cite 4 >}}

## History
The first location to recieve the remodel would be {{ wiki-link "Phoenix, AZ (245 E. Bell Road #55)" }} in April 2013, followed by {{< wiki-link "Mesa, AZ (1906 W Rio Salado Pkwy)">}} in December 2013.{{ cite 1 }}{{ cite 8 }}
