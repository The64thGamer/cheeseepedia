***What I Like About You 2001*** was the main live show for the {{< wiki-link "September 2001 Show" >}}. It is a cover of ['What I Like About You'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/What_I_Like_About_You_(The_Romantics_song)) with Chuck E. singing lead. 

## Summary
The live show consists of Chuck E. singing ‘What I Like About You' with the rest of the band singing backup. The footage is various clips from {{< wiki-link "April 1999" >}}, {{< wiki-link "If You’re Happy & You Know It" >}}, {{< wiki-link "September 2000 Show" >}}, {{< wiki-link "Summertime 2001" >}}, and {{< wiki-link "September 2001 Show" >}}.
