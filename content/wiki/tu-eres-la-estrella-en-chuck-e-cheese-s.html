Cheese's (You Are The Star At Chuck E. Cheese's) is a cassette tape released in the mid-1990's and was only able to be purchased at the Santiago (Mall Plaza Vespucio), Chile and {{< wiki-link "Santiago (Las CondesLo Barnechea), Chile" >}} locations. The cassette featured Chuck E. Live shows from the 1990's dubbed in Spanish.

Playlist

It is currently unknown how many songs were featured on this tape. Birthday Star 1992 and Chuck E. Shuffle are the only current known songs featured on this tape.
