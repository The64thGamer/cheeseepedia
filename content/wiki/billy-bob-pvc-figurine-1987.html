Production

This PVC figurine of Billy Bob would be one of the last new pieces produced of the character while being under ShowBiz Pizza Time.

Set

  • Fatz PVC Figurine (1987)

Design

Specific to this figure, Billy Bob would have eyelashes, no claws on his hands, miss-colored paws, and white claws on his feet. Billy Bob is also seen supported by his guitar standing on a white base with his name written in red at the front of it.
