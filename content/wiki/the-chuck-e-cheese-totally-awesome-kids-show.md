***The Chuck E. Cheese Totally Awesome Kids Show*** was a show that released April 23, 1996 at Chuck E. Cheese's locations. It was the last showtape to be entirely produced by Disney.{{< cite 1 >}}

## Production
On December 30, 2025 demo cuts of various songs would be released by user 'thetrapinquestion' donated by Gabe (Pizzacam) {{< cite 2 >}}. The contents on the cassette are as follows.

- 'Cut 1, Theme - I'm Getting Around'
- 'Cut 2, Segment 1, Travelin' First Class'
- 'Cut 3, Theme 2, I'm Getting Around'
- 'Cut 4, Segment 2, Rollin''
- 'Cut 5, Theme 3, I'm Getting Around'
- 'Cut 6, Segment 3, Pick 'Em Up' or 'Show 2, Segment 3, Pick 'Em Up'
- 'Cut 7, Theme 4, I'm Getting Around'
- 'It's The Chuck E. Cheese Totally Awesome Kids Show' Intro and Exit vocal.
- 'Cut 9, Theme 5, I'm Getting Around'
- 'Cut 10, Segment 5, Don't Drive Me Crazy'
- 'Cut 11, Show Ending Outro Theme'

#### Filming Locations:
- The ending of Segment 5 was filmed at {{< wiki-link "Orlando, FL (7419 International Dr)" >}}.

## Trivia:
- The skateboarding footage in 'Thrillseekers' was previously used for 'Roll With It' in {{< wiki-link "Spring Training" >}}.
- On the Cyberamic Version of the show the One Stage curtains are programed to open and close, after and before the Two Stage Curtains.
