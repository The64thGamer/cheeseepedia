***Summertime 2000*** was a showtape that played through the summer of 2000, themed around Summertime and America.


## Show List

1.  **Segment 1**
    1.  'Summertime'
    2.  'Fun, Fun, Fun'
2.  **Intermission 1**
    1.  Karaoke Spot (Studio C only)
    2.  Wildlife
    3.  Time To Fly! (Studio C)
    4.  Store Intro - 'Brick NJ' (Cookie)
    5.  CEC TV ID - 'Rocket'
3.  **Segment 2**
    1.  'Celebrate America'
    2.  'America'
4.  **Intermission 2**
    1.  Karaoke Spot (Studio C)
    2.  Chuck E.'s Home Movies at The Beach
    3.  CEC TV ID - 'Cows'
    4.  Store Intro - 'Brick NJ' (Steve)
5.  **Segment 3**
    1.  'Good Old Summertime'
    2.  'Local Pizza Cabana' (Parody of ['Copacabana'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Copacabana_(song)))

    1.  Karaoke Spot (Studio C)
    2.  At The Zoo
    3.  CEC TV ID - 'Rocket'
    4.  Store Intro - 'Brick NJ' (Abby)
    5.  Book Your Party Online Promo

## Live Shows

**Live Shows would be on separate VHS tapes for Existing Stage stores.**

- Chuck E. Says
- Chuck E. Shuffle

- Havin' a Party at Chuck E.'s Place
- Birthday Star
- Spanish Birthday
- Chuck E. Swing
- Rockin' Christmas Eve

- Patriotic Live 2000
- Happy and You Know It

**Birthday Break**: Summertime logo with music set to a 1 or 2-minute countdown which would then lead to the Birthday show.

## History

#### Filming Locations

- 'Summertime' and 'Good Ol' Summertime' are filmed at the Fort Worth Japanese & Botanical Gardens
- The lake in 'Row Your Boat' is White Rock Creek in Dallas, Texas.
