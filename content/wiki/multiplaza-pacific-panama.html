It is located on the third floor of Multiplaza Mall.

History

Because of their expansion in Latin America, Chuck E Cheese’s decided to open a location in Panama. The store opened in 2013 to much fanfare, so much so that a second location opened in 2017.

Exterior and Interior

This location is a very early version of the 2013 Update remodel, having a prototype of the International Mall Entrance, and many things that are not from the 2013 Update, including the “Kids” Posters from the {{< wiki-link "CEC 2000's Remodel Program" >}} and the toddler playground from that remodel. The store also has a checkered wall that is actually a window, looking to the road outside the mall.
