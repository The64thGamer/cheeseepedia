***'Rockin’ Christmas Eve'*** was a holiday live show which played alongside {{< wiki-link "Chuck E’s Holiday Party 1999" >}} and was reissued several times after. 

## Summary

The live show is a rock and roll song themed to Christmas Eve. In the middle of the live, Chuck E. invites guests to get out their air guitars so they can jam with Jasper during the guitar solo. At the end, Chuck E. wishes everyone a Merry Christmas and Happy Holidays. The footage shows the band dancing around a Christmas tree, with the aspect ratio switching between 4:3 and 16:9. The birthday break is the Chuck E.’s Holiday Party logo.
