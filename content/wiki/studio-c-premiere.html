DVCAM Footage

The first full footage of the show would be provided by Brian Hagan on December 21, 2021.(1) This was through a DVCAM recording of the show video spliced with recordings of the {{< wiki-link "Studio C" >}} stage performing the showtape. This recording would additionally be AI upscaled. The footage would be copyright-striked minutes after upload, but was able to be downloaded before being pulled. The footage would be reuploaded multiple times with cut down segments, but improved video clarity.(2)
