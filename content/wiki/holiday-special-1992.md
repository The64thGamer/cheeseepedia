Holiday Special 1992 is a Christmas-themed showtape that played during the 1992 holiday season. It was the second Christmas showtape to be produced for Munch's Make Believe Band.
The majority of this show's content would end up being reused for {{< wiki-link "Holiday '93" >}}, which would run from 1993 to 1994.

## Show List:

1. **Intermission 1**
    1.  CEC TV Intro
2. **Segment 1**
    1.  Santa Claus is Coming To Town
    2.  Rudolph The Red-Nosed Reindeer
3. **Intermission 2**
4. **Segment 2**
    1.  Here We Come A Caroling
    2.  Silver Bells
5. **Intermission 3**
6. **Segment 3**
    1.  Share The Joy
    2.  Rockin' Around The Christmas Tree
7. **Intermission 4**

## Trivia

- Share the Joy was originally used in the CEC Xmas '91 showtape. It was redubbed for this year with updated vocals for Helen and Jasper.
