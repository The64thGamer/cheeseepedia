The ***1981 Starcade Tournament*** was a contest held at select Pizza Time Theatres in August of 1981 to poll for contestants to be on the then upcoming Starcade TV show pilot.

## Description

During the first two weeks of August 1981, {{< wiki-link "Huntington Beach, CA (15511 Edwards St)" >}}, {{< wiki-link "Citrus Heights, CA (7614 Greenback Lane)" >}}, {{< wiki-link "San Ramon, CA (1901 San Ramon Valley Blvd)" >}}, {{< wiki-link "Garden Grove, CA (11889 Valley View St)" >}}, {{< wiki-link "Long Beach, CA (4706 Los Coyotes Diagonal)" >}}, {{< wiki-link "San Diego (Mira Mesa), CA (9840 Hibert St)" >}}, {{< wiki-link "Modesto, CA (3037 Sisk Rd)" >}}, {{< wiki-link "Pinole, CA (1570 Fitzgerald Dr)" >}}, {{< wiki-link "Orlando / Winter Park, FL (1300 Lee Rd)" >}}, {{< wiki-link "Tampa, FL (1707 E Fowler Ave)" >}}, {{< wiki-link "Arlington, TX (2216 S Fielder Rd)" >}}, and {{< wiki-link "Fort Worth, TX (8740 Hwy 80 West)" >}} would have customers play arcade games in contests to win a chance at being on the pilot for the upcoming TV show 'Starcade'. Random contestants were also selected from home entry forms that were submitted at these locations.{{< cite 1 >}} The pilot would air on September 13th, 1981, which had all 24 contestants win a party for 10 at their local PTT.{{< cite 2 >}}{{< cite 3 >}}
