This includes an elephant trunk and hooves, duck feet on 1982-1983 models, frog feet on 1981 models, horse hooves, and dog paws. The Clapper Board was on the wall beside Pasqually on the Shelf, Balcony, Rocker, and C-Stage|C-Stages. The last Clapper Board was at either the Lake Forest, California, or Simi Valley, California locations.
