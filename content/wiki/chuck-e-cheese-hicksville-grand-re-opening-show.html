Cheese Hicksville Grand Re-Opening Show is the showtape released on July 19, 2024, to play exclusively for Hicksville Grand Re-Opening event. The show features a variety of songs released from 2014-2023. This show features no intermissions and would play six segments on repeat from around 5:00-7:00pm CT. After the event it would play Northridge’s Summer Show part 2 from 7:00pm-closing.
