The ***Pizza Time Theatre 1979 Animated Spot* was an Unknown Animated Commercial created for Pizza Time Theatre in 1979. The Commercials' plot or contents are unknown besides Multiple Animation Cels showcasing Pasqually, Chuck, and a Kid. {{< cite 1 >}}

## History 
The Animated Commercial was developed by an unknown company, possibly PTT Inc themselves in 1979. The Commercial may be the Animated 30-second Birthday Commercial that aired on local California Stations during Late 1979. {{< cite 2 >}} It is Unknown if the Animated Ad aired if it is not the Birthday Commercial however, and even then was only shortly used before the beggining of 1980.

The history of the Animation Cels is mostly unknown, but several are now in a private collection.
