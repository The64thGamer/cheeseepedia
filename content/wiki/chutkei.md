***出奇老鼠薄餅樂園*** (roughly translating to ***Chuck E. Mouse's Pizza Paradise***, though the first word 出奇 'Chūqí' sounding like 'Chuck E.' more directly translates to 'Surprising', and also usually marked in english as just ***Chuck E. Cheese's***) was a chain of five {{< wiki-link "Pizza Time Theatre" >}} restaruants situated within Hong Kong, Tsuen Wan, Kowloon, and Singapore.
