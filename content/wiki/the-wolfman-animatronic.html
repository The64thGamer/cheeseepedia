He is meant to be a parody of the radio DJ, Wolfman Jack. Wolfman's mask uses the same mold as {{< wiki-link "Buddy Crash (Animatronic)" >}} from the {{< wiki-link "Bear Country Jubilee" >}}, with teeth and mustache glued on. His mech was later reused for Repete Thornsberry in the {{< wiki-link "Hard Luck Bears" >}}.
