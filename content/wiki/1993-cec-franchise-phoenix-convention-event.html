History

The event would be held at the Phoenician Resort (Scottsdale, AZ, 6000 E Camelback Rd).(1) The convention would host a industrial musical, which featured an exclusive song produced by Scott Wilson for the event, being 'Escapade' sung by Helen Henny.(2)
