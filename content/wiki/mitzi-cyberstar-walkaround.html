The walkaround was produced by J&M Costumers, alongside the other costumes used in the Cyberstar videos.(1)

Variants

The First version was a pretty accurate Mitzi. The way you would be able to see out of the costume was through the eyes and possibly nose and mouth. The Second version was the one that actually got used. In this version however, Mitzi's face is alot more rounder and her eyes are bigger and blue. Her ears don't stay up like the other one.
