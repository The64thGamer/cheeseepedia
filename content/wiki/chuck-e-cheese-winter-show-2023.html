Cheese Winter Show 2023 is a holiday show released on November 27, 2023 and was played until January 1, 2024. This show features six Holiday songs, of which all were reused. The standard live show is, 'Chuck E.'s Yuletide Slide'. The 'Birthday Extravaganza' live would also be updated to have an American Sign Language variant usable.
