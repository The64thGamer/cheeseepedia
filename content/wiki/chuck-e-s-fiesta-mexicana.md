***Chuck E.'s Fiesta Mexicana*** is a generic live show released alongside {{< wiki-link "Chuck E's Tall Tales" >}} which could be played through December 2001. 

## Summary

The show starts with an intro featuring the Chuckettes along with some kids and presumably a character named Uncle Simon. The live show features Chuck E. singing in English, while the rest of the band sings in Spanish. At the end, the kids and Chuckettes thank Chuck E. and Uncle Simon for teaching them the dance. 

## Live Show List:

1. 'Chuck E.'s Fiesta Mexicana' (parody of ['Mexican hat dance'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mexican_hat_dance))
2. Birthday Break
3. {{< wiki-link "Birthday Star '95" >}}

## Studio C

The Studio C version of this live show does not include the intro and outro with the Chuckettes, instead only including the song itself.
