Cheese Northridge Evergreen Show 2023 is the generic show released on November 11, 2023 - November 26, 2023 featuring two songs from 2017. This was created as a unique showtape to only be played at Northridge to preserve the animatronics. This show features 2 Songs with the standard live being Me & My Friends.
