The showtape was presumably sent alongside the show to the Family Entertainment Center that purchased it from South America.

IAAPA Debut

The showtape would debut at the IAAPA 1995 convention along with the Toyland stage itself. To the left of the stage was the New RAE Lite-CS with Karaoke and NRAE Mini Stage. Each show would play a single segment before passing it onto the next stage. The only footage of this showtape during the convention comes from Brian Hagan, who uploaded it onto YouTube on December 18, 2021, featuring Segment 13: Hot Fun in the Summertime.
