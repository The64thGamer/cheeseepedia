***Chuck E. Cheese Show Reimagined*** is a series of songs re-programmed by Donovan Marcelle on the 3-Stage using {{< wiki-link "Animation Programming System" >}}. In total he would do 14 songs/segments.

## Trivia
- 'When You're Here' is originally from {{< wiki-link "Let The Good Times Roll" >}} Segment 4.
- 'Turn the Beat Around' is originally from {{< wiki-link "August 1996 Show" >}} Segment 3.
- 'These Hands' is originally from {{< wiki-link "Working For A Living" >}} Segment 3.
- 'Take Me Out To The Ballgame' is originally from {{< wiki-link "Spring Training" >}} Segment 2.
- 'Shake Your Grove Thing' is originally from {{< wiki-link "Fall 2012 Show" >}} Segment 3.
- 'Dancing On The Moon' is originally from {{< wiki-link "CEC Rock It In Space" >}} Segment 3.
- 'Party Rock' is originally from {{< wiki-link "Spring 2012 Show" >}} Segment 1.
- 'In The Still Of The Night' and 'Midnight Special' are originally from {{< wiki-link "Spring Training" >}} Segment 3.
- 'In My Pool' is originally from {{< wiki-link "Summertime 2003" >}} Segment 2.
- 'I've Been Working On The Railroad' is originally from {{< wiki-link "Working For A Living" >}} Segment 1.
- 'The Train Song' (Train Ride) is originally from {{< wiki-link "Planes, Trains, And Automobiles" >}} Segment 1.
- 'I'm So Excited' and 'Baby You're A Star' are originally from {{< wiki-link "You're A Star" >}} Segment 3.
- 'I Feel Good' is originally from {{< wiki-link "July 1993 Show" >}} Segment 3.
- 'I Fall To Pieces' is originally from {{< wiki-link "You're A Star" >}} Segment 3.
- 'Feel The Rhythm' and 'Do You Believe In Magic' are originally from {{< wiki-link "July 1993 Show" >}} Segment 2.
- 'Escapade' is originally from {{< wiki-link "1993 CEC Franchise Phoenix Convention (Event)" >}} Song 14.
- 'Come See About Me' is originally from {{< wiki-link "April 2006 Show" >}} Segment 3.

- Programming for 'In My Pool' was originally done in 2018 for {{< wiki-link "Chuck E. Cheese's April International Show 2018" >}}
- Programming for 'These Hands' began on August 3, 2022 during a live stream where Donovan had the audience guess what song it was. It wasn't till August 16, 2022 it was discovered what song was being programmed{{< cite 1 >}}. On June 26, 2024 'These Hands' and the rest of the songs would be uploaded onto YouTube{{< cite 2 >}}
