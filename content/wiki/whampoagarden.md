***Hung Hom, Hong Kong, China (Whampoa Garden Phase 1)*** was a {{< wiki-link "Chuck E. Mouse's Pizza Paradise (出奇老鼠薄餅樂園)" >}} that opened around December 1985 to 1986, and closed at an unknown time.

## History
The opening of the location can be narrowed down between 1985-1986, due to an advertisement showing it alongside other then-open locations. Whampoa Garden Phase 1 would begin occupancy in December of 1985, with this likely being when the location opened.{{< cite 2 >}}

After closure, the location would be turned into a Happy Land by its owners. {{< cite 1 >}}
