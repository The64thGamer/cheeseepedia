***Sunnyvale, CA (155 Moffett Park Drive)*** was the Atari Consumer Division (Pizza Time Theatre's) corporate headquarters from January 1976 to February, 1979. [1] This was paired alongside the manufacturing offices at [Sunnyvale, CA (1195 Borregas Avenue)].
