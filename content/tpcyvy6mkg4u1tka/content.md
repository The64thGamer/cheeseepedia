The ***Jingle Bell Jamboree*** (Stylized as JINGLEBELL JAMBOREE) was an Animatronic Show at Santa's Village in Jefferson, New Hampshire from 1977 until it's Removal in 2016. The Show was originally built by Creative Engineering as it's first full show, but was later changed to have Advanced Animations Animatronics from 2009 to 2010.

## History

### Production

The Show originally began production in 1977 as 'The Santa Claus Revue'[1] by Creative Engineering. The Concept Art showcased Left stage with Rudolph, Center Stage with the 3 Elves, Right Stage with Santa, and Elfis and Ms Claus on the Side Walls. The Show was created for Santa's Village unlike previous CEI Animatronics & Attractions.

### Creative Engineering

Jingle Bell Jamboree was installed at an unknown time in 1977 in Santa's Village's Jingle Bell Theater[2]. The Show differed from Concept Art, scrapping Ms Claus and having Elfis be in the Center of Center Stage after Rudolph's Segment using a Trap Door. The Show also installed 3 small Animal Animatronics. The Show was originally installed with Three 8-Track Reels, but CEI later took a private flight to Santa's Village in 1979 to install a new CEI 4-Track Reel Showtape as a sign of appreciation[2].
The Show eventually began to break down, and began removal in 2006[3].

### Advanced Animations

Santa's Village would get Advanced Animation's to redo the Show alongside the Outside Rudolph Head which was also originally developed by Creative Engineering, Creating Concept art and Building the Animatronics between 2009-2010. In 2009, The Show was retrofitted into a Half Animatronic Half Live show called the 'Jingle Bell Talent Show'[4]. The Talent Show only Used Rudolph and Elfin John's animatronic retrofitted into an unknown Elf character.

The Show was finished and installed in 2010, using the original showtape. The Show removed the 3 Smaller Animals, and later removed the Red backdrop on the Top Art. The Show continued to run until 2016, in which it was removed.

## Design

### Overall Stage


The Overall stage featured a wooden base with curved panels and two metal grates on each end of center stage. The stage also featured red curtains and garlands that were placed around the edges of the stage, the stage floor, and on the valence. Two JBL 4311B Speakers were placed between the stages with two large candy canes sitting on top. Above the stage was a solid valence that featured red fabric and a fluffy white trim. On the side stages, there were elf faces that represented the Comedy and Tragedy masks often found within Theatre. Stage Right - Tragedy, Stage Left - Comedy. Surrounding the elf faces was a wreath with two candy canes framing it on both sides. Center stage featured a gradient from red to black, with the black acting as the night sky, decorated with rhinestones that acted as the stars, with some forming the North Star in the middle of the valence. On this was Santa riding his Sleigh with a fabric bag of presents, being pulled by 5 Reindeer with two elves holding onto a rope hanging off the back of the Sleigh. Attached to this was fabric valence pieces that would swoop down in circles.

### Left Stage

The Show's Left Stage contained Rudolph on with a Black Backdrop.

### Center Stage

Center Stage contained Multiple Animatronics. From left to right the Stage had a gray Dog, Elfin John on piano, a white Rabbit on the piano, Satchmo on the trumpet, Maynard on Bass, and a black/dark gray Cat.

The Stage contained a Custom Carpet with a smaller red Carpet on top of it in the center. The Stage also contained Stockings and a Fire place under a Picture, alongside a Large Christmas Tree.

Elfis would come out of a black trap door.

the Show later removed and changed many of these features including removing the Animals and changing Elfin John's Piano's design. The Backdrop was changed to replace the picture, added presents, removed the stockings, removed the tree, and added façade windows. The Floor also removed the old carpet for a black carpet.

### Right Stage

Right stage contained Santa on a Chair with a Black Backdrop.
