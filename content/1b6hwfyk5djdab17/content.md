***Chuck E. Cheese Shows (CEC Best of 1988)*** was released for *Chuck E. Cheese's* locations in 1989 and featured four segments reused from previous showtapes, with two of the segments having newly recorded video.

    1. Tune Machine 1
        1. ['Swinging on a Star'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swinging_on_a_Star)

2. **Segment 2**
    1. Tune Machine 2
        1. ['Camelot'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Camelot_(musical))
