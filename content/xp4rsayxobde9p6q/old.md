***222 E Main Street*** was a Pizza Time Theater in New Britain, Connecticut that opened in July 1983 with a [Cyberamics Balcony Stage] and closed in 1985. At the entrance, It had a Rat Hole for the Kids to experience going in.

## Stages

When they were opened, they only had a [Cyberamics Balcony Stage]. After closure, the bots were either destroyed, sent to another location, or placed in storage.
