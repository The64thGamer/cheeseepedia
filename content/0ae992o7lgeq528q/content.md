The ***Peter Piper Pizza 2.0 Remodel Program*** is a remodel initiative for [Peter Piper Pizza] locations that started in April of 2013 with test locations and a logo redesign, before the company would be aquired by [Chuck E. Cheese's] in 2014 and the final remodel being completed and pushed nationwide in 2016 around the same time as the [CEC 2.0 Remodel Program].[1][2][4]

## History
The first location to recieve the remodel would be [Phoenix, AZ (245 E. Bell Road #55)] in April 2013, followed by [Mesa, AZ (1906 W Rio Salado Pkwy)] in December 2013.[1][8]
