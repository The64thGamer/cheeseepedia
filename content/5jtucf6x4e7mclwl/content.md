The ***Fantasy Forest Victorville, CA Office*** (15367 Tamarack Unit B, Victorville, CA 92892) was the home offices and manufacturing facility for [Fantasy Forest] (Originally known as Fantasy Forest Road Shows[1]) from 1971 to an unknown year (latest being 1981).[4]
[2]
The location is commonly referred to as the Mountain View Acres offices, despite being located just outside the designated border of the CDP area.[3]
