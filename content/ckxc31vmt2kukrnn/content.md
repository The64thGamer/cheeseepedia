***Christmas Live*** was an unauthorized showtape produced by Creative Engineering as a gift for employees of *Showbiz Pizza Place* locations in 1986.

## Show List:

1. **Segment 1**
    1. 'Deck the Halls'
    2. Manager Recognition
    3. Spirit of Christmas Skit
    4. 'Deck the Halls'
    5. Goodbye Skit

## Production:

This showtape was created as an act of rebellion by Creative Engineering in 1986, after Showbiz Pizza Time Inc began producing showtapes for the Rock-afire Explosion in-house. The showtape was quickly produced entirely ad-libbed by the CEI voice cast. It would then be packaged with a Christmas card and sent out to all stores without permission from Corporate. This show was created to be played once after hours for employees and the manager, and then taken home by the manager as a keepsake.
