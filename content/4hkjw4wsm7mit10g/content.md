***Irving, TX (4560 Regent Blvd, Floor 1)*** is the current corporate headquarters of [Chuck E. Cheese's], starting its use in June of 2026.


## History

The earliest known evidence for CEC to potentially move from the previous headquarters at [Irving, TX (4441 W Airport Fwy Ste 215)] came in January of 2026, when it was discovered in CEC's SEC filings that the lease for the previous corporate offices expired June of 2026. By April the move was confirmed through internal leaks and set for the office building just down the street, with the note that some items would begin moving immediately to the Texas warehouse, while the full move would occur on June 22nd.[2] By early May a search at the address would not see any direct signs of a move-in at the property.[3] The earliest known photo of CEC staff in the new headquarters would be within the week of July 6th, 2026.[4]
