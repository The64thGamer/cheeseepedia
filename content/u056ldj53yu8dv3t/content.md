***Let’s Groove*** was the main live show for the {{< wiki-link "January 2004 Show" >}}. It is a cover of ['Let’s Groove'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Let%27s_Groove) with Chuck E. singing lead. 

## Summary
The live show consists of Chuck E. singing ‘Let's Groove' with the rest of the band singing backup. The main footage is animated text saying ‘Chuck E. Live’ with animated backgrounds.
