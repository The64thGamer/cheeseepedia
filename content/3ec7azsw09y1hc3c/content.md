***What I Like About You 2001*** was the main live show for the [September 2001 Show]. It is a cover of ['What I Like About You'](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/What_I_Like_About_You_(The_Romantics_song)) with Chuck E. singing lead. 

## Summary
The live show consists of Chuck E. singing ‘What I Like About You' with the rest of the band singing backup. The footage is various clips from [April 1999], [If You’re Happy & You Know It], [September 2000 Show], [Summertime 2001], and [September 2001 Show].
