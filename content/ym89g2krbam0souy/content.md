***Christmas 86 Edited*** was released for *Showbiz Pizza Place* locations in 1986, featuring a collection of reused Holiday material from early 80s shows.

## Show List:

1. **Segment 1**
    1. 'Silent Night'
    2. 'Feliz Navidad'
2. **Segment 2**
    1. 'T'was the Night Before Christmas'
3. **Segment 3**
    1. 'Why Can't Every Day Be Christmas'
4. **Segment 4**
    1. '12 Days of Christmas'
5. **Segment 5**
    1. 'In December'
