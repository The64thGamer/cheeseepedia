***Tu Eres La Estrella En Chuck E. Cheese's*** (You Are The Star At Chuck E. Cheese's) is a cassette tape released in the mid-1990's and was only able to be purchased at the Santiago (Mall Plaza Vespucio), Chile and [Santiago (Las CondesLo Barnechea), Chile] locations.
The cassette featured the Spanish version of Birthday Star 1992, alongside a sing-along version of the song.

## Track List:

1. **Track 1**
    1.  Chuck E. Intro
2. **Track 2**
    1.  [Spanish Birthday '92]
3. **Track 3**
    1.  [Spanish Birthday '92] (Karaoke)
4. **Track 4**
    1.  Spanish [Chuck E. Shuffle] Commercial
