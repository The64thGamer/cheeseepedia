***15225 W 134th St, Store #96* is a *Chuck E. Cheese* that opened on May 3, 2002 with a [Studio C Alpha], and is still open to this day with the [CEC 2.0 Remodel Program].**

## Stages

Olathe, Kansas opened with the Studio C Alpha in May of 2002, before being replaced by the [Chuck E Live Stage (Dance Floor)] in August of 2017.
