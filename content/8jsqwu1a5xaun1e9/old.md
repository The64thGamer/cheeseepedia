***Houston, TX (12523 Westheimer Rd)* is a *Chuck E. Cheese* that open on October 22, 2012 with a [Circles of Light], and is still open to this day with the [CEC 2.0 Remodel Program]. This location was a relocation of the [Houston, TX (14637 Memorial Dr)] location, which opened on August 26, 1981, as a *ShowBiz Pizza Place* with a [Classic Stage], and closed on October 24, 2012, as a *Chuck E. Cheese's* with a 3-Stage.**
