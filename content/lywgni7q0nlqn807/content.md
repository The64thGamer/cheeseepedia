The ***Cheese Roll*** (not to be confused with the {{< wiki-link "Chuck E. Cheese Roll" >}}) was a rebrand of *High Ball* released by The Norton Co. in Pizza Time Theatres starting around 1983. High Ball was available with various different marquees for each vendor.

## History

The Cheese Roll would appear at the AOE '83 exhibit from March 26-27, 1983 in the form of an alternate marquee shown off at the High Ball portion of the Norton Co. booth.{{< cite 6 >}}
