***Zip-A-Dee-Doo-Dah Performed by Helen Henny*** was a segment programmed at CEC Corporate by Donovan Marcelle for fun.
The segment's audio was the song 'Zip-A-Dee-Doo-Dah' by Patti Austin{{< cite 1 >}}, and programmed by Donovan on the 3-Stage using {{< wiki-link "Animation Programming System" >}}.
