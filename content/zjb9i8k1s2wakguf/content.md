***Kowloon / Tsim Sha Tsui East, Hong Kong, China (96 Granville Rd.)*** was the first {{< wiki-link "Chuck E. Mouse's Pizza Paradise (出奇老鼠薄餅樂園)" >}} and the 200th {{< wiki-link "Pizza Time Theatre" >}} location, running from December of 1982 to 1985-1986.

## History

The location would be run by the Whimsy Company, alongside the Modern Advertising Company running advertisements. The location would functionally not change from other PTT stores outside of having shrimp pizza and spaghetti served.{{< cite 1 >}} Other locations in Hong Kong and Singapore would open by the Whimsy Company after the succcess of the first location.
