**All:** 

We wish you a merry Christmas. 

We wish you a merry Christmas. 

We wish you a merry Christmas and a happy new year

**Chuck E. Cheese:** 

‘Twas the night before Christmas, 

when all through the store, 

not a creature was sleeping

**All:** 

Cause Munch likes to snore

**Mr. Munch:** 

Cut it out, you guys

**Chuck E. Cheese:** 

Helen and Jasper were just laying back 

and good old Pasqually had whipped up a snack.

Then I grabbed some tokens and everyone came 

with pizza and drinks for a nice friendly game.

When out in the lot there arose such a clatter, 

we stopped whacking munches to see what was the matter. 

Away to the front door we flew like a flash, 

bumped into each other

**All:** 

And fell with a crash

**Chuck E. Cheese:** 

Then Jasper looked up and yelled

**Jasper T. Jowls:** 

What do you know, the whole parking lot is covered with snow

**Chuck E. Cheese:** 

Then we all ran to see but we couldn’t because the doorway was blocked by my friend

**All:** 

Santa Claus

**Chuck E. Cheese:** 

He looked to be tired like a man with jet lag. 

His eyes didn’t sparkle and his feet seemed to drag.

 He said he was almost through dropping off his toys 

to all of the world's best behaved girls and boys

**Santa:** 

But Chuck E., I’m hungry and a little tired too. 

Do you think I could rest and share a pizza with you?

**Chuck E. Cheese:** 

So we brought him a drink and a slice with the works. 

Which he ate in a second than turned with a jerk. 

And his sack of presents was suddenly there. 

And Santa was pulling out presents to spare. 

A derby for Chuck E., 

some pans for Pasqually, 

for Helen, a bonnet all trimmed up with holly, 

for Jasper, a beautiful little brown pup

**Santa:** 

And this is for Munch if he ever wakes up. 

And I’ve got one more present for you guys tonight because you make every day happy and bright

**Chuck E. Cheese:** 

And then we heard singing a beautiful song, 

and into the restaurant marching along

came dozens of friends. 

People like you who 

make each day at Chuck E.’s a dream coming true.

The noise woke up Munch,

 who came in with a yawn. 

I told him “Here’s Santa” 

But Santa was gone. 

Munch opened his present and said with a tear

**Mr. Munch:** 

With friends like you, Christmas can last all the year

**Chuck E. Cheese:** 

Then he grabbed him a pizza, 

for ? cheese gave a whistle 

and the whole thing disappeared 

like the down of a thistle. 

Then he said as he wiped off his hands on his shirt

**Mr. Munch:** 

Happy Christmas to all now

**All:**

What’s for dessert?

**Chuck E. Cheese:** 

We wish you a merry Christmas

**All:** 

We wish you a merry Christmas. 

We wish you a merry Christmas and a happy new year.

Merry Christmas
