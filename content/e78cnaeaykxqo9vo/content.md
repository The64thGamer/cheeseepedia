The ***Beach Bear Diagnostic Tape* is a 1981 [Rock-afire Explosion] diagnostic showtape that used the [CEI Betamax] format to display video for reference in adjustments.[1]
The tape was part of a diagnostic package also containing the Billy Bob Diagnostic Tape and Rolfe & Earl Diagnostic Tape.**
