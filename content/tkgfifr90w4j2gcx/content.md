***Ronald McDonald* was an animatronic created in 1984 by Setmakers and AVG Technologies for the [Ronald McDonald in a Hot Air Balloon] display in McDonalds locations.
It is unknown how many were produced or how long they lasted in the stores. The only confirmed installation was at a Glendale, Coloado store in 1984.**

## Movements

- Head Left
- Head Right
- Left Arm Raise
- Right Arm Raise
- Blink
