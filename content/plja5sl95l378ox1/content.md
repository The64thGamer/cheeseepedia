***Kansas City, MO (2726 NE Vivion Rd)*** (Commonly referred to as '***Antioch***') was a {{< wiki-link "Chuck E Cheese's" >}} that opened on March 2nd {{< cite 1 >}}, 1980 as the first {{< wiki-link "Showbiz Pizza Place" >}} in The Antioch Mall and closed sometime from May 2007 to August 2008 for the Opening of {{< wiki-link "Kansas City, MO (9196 N Skyview Ave)" >}} on August 6th, 2008 {{< cite 2 >}}.

## History

### Pre-Opening

#### Pizza Time Theatre

***Kansas City, MO (2726 NE Vivion Rd)*** was originally planned to be developed as the ninth {{< wiki-link "Pizza Time Theatre" >}} by {{< wiki-link "Pizza Show Biz" >}} under Topeka Inn Management alongside {{< wiki-link "Overland Park, KS (10510 Metcalf Lane)" >}} {{< cite 3 >}} & {{< wiki-link "Jacksonville, FL (9820 Atlantic Blvd)" >}}.
The Store was first officially announces on October 10th, 1979, planned to open in February 1980 {{< cite 4 >}}. For Hire adverts were posted in Newspapers from November to December 1979 {{< cite 5 >}}. It's known the Store was fully or mostly built prior to any name change. In January 1980, Bob Brock would visit {{< wiki-link "Creative Engineering" >}}, later forming an agreement to house the {{< wiki-link "Wolf Pack 5" >}} in Antioch {{< cite 6 >}}. The Last mention of the PTT name in the Center is From February 24th, 1980 {{< cite 7 >}}. It's unknown when the store officially broke off from PTT.

#### Showbiz Pizza Place

Antioch would be relisted as an upcoming Pizza Show Biz in January 1980 alongside the opening date being set to March 2nd {{< cite 7 >}}. Sometime around February 1980, The Showbiz Pizza Place name would be finalized {{< cite 8 >}}. On February 24th, the Kansas City Star would publish an article covering Showbiz Pizza Place prior to Opening {{< cite 9 >}}, alongside info about CEI and Aaron Fechter. The Article would contain info about the planned opening date, The Characters (Minus Beach Bear), CEI information, and planned future locations.
