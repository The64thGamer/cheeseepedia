The ***Crow (Animatronic)*** was an animatronic character built by [Creative Engineering] in 1995 for the [CEI 1995 IAAPA Show (Event)] which took place between november 15th and 18th [1]

## History

The Crow was built in 1995 [2] proven by the [New Rock-afire Explosion] stage at the convention with it being introduced in 1995 [3]

The bot is a partial retrofit of the [Click / Murray D. Bird (Animatronic)] reusing the main latex portion of the head/body and beak.Despite this it's belived to have a mostly if not entirely unique mech due to Click's mech extending deep into his clock.In the only footage of the Crow in action he can be seen singing along with the [Bubba The Gorilla (Animatronic)] to the song "You May Be Right",originally from the [Spring Shows '82] showtape,but was then reused for multiple of the showtapes for the [Bubba The Gorilla (Animatronic)] .It's belived the Crow's mouth movement in the fooatge may have run on the same signal as the mouth movement of the [Woody the Tree (Animatronic)] specifically from the [Bubba + Tree Showtape] .

<br>
The Crow stood on a horizontal red painted metal pipe mounted into the scaffolding for the sign of [Creative Engineering] 's booth.Pointing inwards to look in the same direction as Bubba.

The bot would not be sold and was sent back to [Orlando, FL (47 W Jefferson St)] where he would reside for many years being placed on the base of the [Clown (Animatronic)] on his right side [5]

## Current Whereabouts

The exact whereabouts of the original Crow are unknown

## Design

The Crow is a black bird with a yellow beak,large oval white eyes with small black cross eyed pupils.It has 2 thin yellow feet with 3 digits
