***3160 W Ridge Rd*, was a Chuck E. Cheese's Pizza Time Theater that opened on March 15th, 1983 , with a [Cyberamics Balcony Stage] and closed in November 1996 (day unknown), as a Chuck E. Cheese's with a [2-Stage].
This location should not be confused with the Rochester, NY (3130 Ridge Rd) location, which opened on July 5th, 2003 with a [Studio C Beta], and is still open to this day with the 2.0 remodel.**
