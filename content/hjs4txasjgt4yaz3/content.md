The ***Pizza Time Theatre Basic Ordering Agreement* is a partially lost group of early [Pizza Time Theatre] documents detailing the early concepts of the company and its first two locations: [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)] and [San Jose, CA (1371 Kooser Rd)], likely dating between 1976-1977.
The documents are currently in possession by Gene Landrum, and only a few photos of their contents have surfaced.[1]**

### Ordering

Notably, all the pages seem to be put together by date. Page 3, while not denoting a specific date, does not give the cat character a name, which was later named sometime by the [The Big Cheese Birthday Show Dialogue Recording Session (October 7, 1976)] in October. The only outlier is Page 35, which details the costs of assembling animation hardware for the [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)] location- though it being of financials may have it as part of a separate financial section of the documents.
