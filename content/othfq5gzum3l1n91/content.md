The ***Helen Henny Showtape*** is a currently lost *Pizza Time Theatre* showtape that debuted at the [San Jose, CA (370 S Winchester Blvd)] in August of 1977.

## History

The confirmation of this showtapes existence is from the installation of [Winchester Helen Henny (Animatronic)] at the Winchester location on August 12, 1977.[1] The showtape would run until [Madame Oink I] replaced it in February of 1978.
