***Tampa, FL (2363 E. Fowler Ave)*** was a [Pizza Time Theatre] that opened on May 5th, 1981, before closing in early 1983 to be reopened as [Zapp's Bar & Grill] by May of 1983. The location would stay open until an unknown date.

## History

### Zapp's
The earliest mention of the place reopened as a [Zapp's Bar & Grill] was on May 11th, 1984 in an ad.[4]

The last public mention of the location would be on July 8th, 1985.[3]
