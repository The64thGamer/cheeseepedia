**Waterbury, CT (983 Wolcott St)** was a Pizza Time Theatre that opened in April 1982 and closed in 1984. It was at auction before everything was thrown out. This location is not to be confused with the [Waterbury, CT (650 Wolcott St)] location, which opened on September 29, 1992, and closed on June 25, 2020. This location was the first in the New England region. It was located in the 2 Brothers Plaza. It had a [Cyberamics Balcony Stage], with only a few photos of it. As of March 2024, the location is a Goodwill Waterbury Store &amp; Donation Station.

## Stages

Waterbury opened with a [Cyberamics Balcony Stage] which stayed until closure. The animatronics are unknown to what happened with them after closure.
