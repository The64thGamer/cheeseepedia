***5612 Durand Ave*** is a *Chuck E. Cheese’s* that opened on January 2nd, 2001 with a [Studio C Alpha]. They received the 2.0 remodel in 2019. It's located in a freestanding building in the Regency Mall.

## History

Racine opened on January 2nd, 2001 in a freestanding building in the Regency Mall with a [Studio C Alpha] with the 2000s update of the [SPT 1990's Remodel Program]. This was one of 3 Alphas installed in Wisconsin. In 2019 they received the 2.0 remodel removing the Studio C Alpha for a 2019 Model Dance Floor. Its grand opening was on December 10th, 2019. As of November 2023, they remain like this.
