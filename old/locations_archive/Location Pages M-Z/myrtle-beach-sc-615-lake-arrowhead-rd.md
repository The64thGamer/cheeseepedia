***Myrtle Beach, SC (615 Lake Arrowhead Rd)*** was a *ShowBiz Pizza Place* that open on June 13, 1983 with a [Classic Stage], and closed as a *Chuck E. Cheese's* with a 3-Stage on October 26, 1997.
