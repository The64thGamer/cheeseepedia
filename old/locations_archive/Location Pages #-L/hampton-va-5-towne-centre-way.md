***5 Towne Center Way* is a *Chuck E. Cheese's* that opened in May 2002 (day unknown) with a [Studio C Beta].
This was the relocation of [Hampton, VA (4027 W Mercury Blvd)] which opened in May 1982 (day unknown) as a Pizza Time Theater with a [Cyberamics Balcony Stage] and Closed in May 2002 with a [2-Stage].**
