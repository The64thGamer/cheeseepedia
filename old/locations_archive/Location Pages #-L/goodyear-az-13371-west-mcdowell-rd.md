***Goodyear AZ (13371 West McDowell Rd)*** is a *Chuck E. Cheese* that opened in November 2009 with a [Studio C Beta]. The animatronic came from [Scottsdale, AZ (8890 E Indian Bend Rd)]. In March 2023, the location would receive the [CEC 2.0 Remodel Program].

## Stages

| Stage                                   | Installed     | Removed       |
|-----------------------------------------|---------------|---------------|
| [Studio C Beta] | November 2009 | March 2023    |
| Dance Floor + Video Wall                | March 2023    | Still in use. |
