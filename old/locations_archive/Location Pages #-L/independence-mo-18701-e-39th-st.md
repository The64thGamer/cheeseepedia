***18701 E 39th St*** is a *Chuck E. Cheese's* that opened on May 2012 (day unknown) and is still open today with a [Chuck E Live Stage (Dance Floor)] + [Video Wall] and the 2.0 remodel. This was the relocation of [Independence, MO (4039 S Noland Rd)], which opened on October 14, 1980 as a ShowBiz Pizza, and closed in May 2012 (day unknown) with a [Concept Unification Stage].

## Stages

Independence has had a [Studio C Beta], 16m. Starting with this store, the Beta was mixed with elements of the Small-Town Studio C|Studio C Cappa. On 9/16/2024 the stage got fully removed and is going to be replaced with the [Chuck E Live Stage (Dance Floor)] + [Video Wall].

| Stage                                        | Installed      | Removed           | Notes                                                             |
|----------------------------------------------|----------------|-------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|
| [Studio C Beta], 16m | May 2012       | September 16 2024 | Last Beta in Missouri. Last Animatronic of any kind in the state. |
| Dance Floor + Video Wall                     | September 2024 | Still in use.     |                                                                   |

## History

Independence opened in May 2012 (day unknown) as the relocation of the old store. It has the 2010 update of the 2003 'Cool Chuck' Reconfiguration / Major Remodel Initiative, which included multiple things, like [Skytubes] and the Beta itself. Much has changed as now the store is now a 2.0 store.
