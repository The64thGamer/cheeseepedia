The **Chuck E. Cheese's Corporate Office** is the current headquarters used by CEC Entertainment. They moved into the building in July 2015, after moving from the old [ShowBiz Pizza Time Corporate Office]. The building is located at 1707 Market Pl Blvd #200, Irving, TX 75063, in the Lennar DFW Division Office.
