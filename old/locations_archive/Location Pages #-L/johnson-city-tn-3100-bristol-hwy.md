***Johnson City, TN (3100 Bristol Hwy)*** was a ShowBiz Pizza Place that opened in January 1986 with a Rock-Afire Explosion and closed in 2001 with the 3-Stage. This is not to be confused with Johnson City, TN (3020 Peoples St) which opened on March 17th, 2005, with a [Studio C Cappa] and currently has the 2.0 Remodel.

## Stages

Originally when the store opened, they had a Rock-Afire Explosion, as all other ShowBiz locations did. They would have it for a few years, until around 1991-1992, when they would undergo Concept Unification, this was the process used to turn the RAE into the CEC characters. In the Mid 1990's Chuck's mask was replaced with a newer one. The store would remain this way until closure.

| [Concept Unification Stage] | 1991-1992    | 2001      | One of eight Tennessee locations to get Concept Unification.           |
