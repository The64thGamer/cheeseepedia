***700 Exterior Street*** is a *Chuck E. Cheese's* that opened on December 26, 2013 with a [Circles of Light], and is still open to this day with a 2017 Model [Chuck E Live Stage (Dance Floor)]. This location opened on the same day as [Port Orange, FL (5539 S Williamson Blvd)], El Paso, TX (1910 N Zaragoza Rd), and [West Palm Beach, FL (2471 Okeechobee Blvd)].

## History

Bronx opened on December 26, 2013 with a [Circles of Light]. Around 2015-2016, the store would replace it with a [Chuck E Live Stage (Dance Floor)].
