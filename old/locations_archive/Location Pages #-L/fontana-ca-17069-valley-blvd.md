***Fontana, CA (17069 Valley Blvd), Store #445*** was a *Pizza Time Theatre* that opened in November 1983 with a [Cyberamics Balcony Stage], and is still open to this day with the [CEC 2.0 Remodel Program].

## History

Fontana opened in November 1983 with a [Cyberamics Balcony Stage], which was to the right of the entrance. In 1988/89, they got the [SPT 1980's Remodel Program], retrofitting the Balcony Stage to [Chuck E's House Facade]. Later in 1994/95, they received the SPT 1992/1993 Remodel Program, and got a [2-Stage]. Then in late 2006, they received the [CEC 2000's Remodel Program], relocated the showroom, and got a [Studio C Beta]. In April 2023, they received the [CEC 2.0 Remodel Program] removing the Studio C for a [Chuck E Live Stage (Dance Floor)] + [Video Wall]. As of October 2023, Fontana remains like this.
