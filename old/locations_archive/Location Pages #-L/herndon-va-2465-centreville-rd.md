***2465 Centreville Rd, Store #368*** is a Chuck E. Cheese's that opened on May 19, 1992, with a [2-Stage], and is still open to this day with the [CEC 2.0 Remodel Program].

## History

Herndon opened on May 19, 1992. At first, they had a [2-Stage]. Nothing special is known about it, and stayed up until the location received the [CEC 2000's Remodel Program] in 2005-2007, replacing the [2-Stage] with a [Studio C Beta]. It would remain this way until July 2023, when the location received the [CEC 2.0 Remodel Program], meaning the Studio C was removed for a [Chuck E Live Stage (Dance Floor)] + [Video Wall]. The Avenger Chuck sign remained, but was replaced with a front with a green outfit. The location also received a Trampoline Zone. As of September 2023, Herndon remains like this.

| [Studio C Beta]                                                           | 2005-2007 | July 2023    |
| [Chuck E Live Stage (Dance Floor)] + [Video Wall] | July 2023 | Still In Use |
