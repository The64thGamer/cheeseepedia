***Toledo, OH (5455 Airport Hwy), Store #554*** is a *Chuck E. Cheese's* that opened on September 6th, 1993 with a {{< wiki-link "1-Stage" >}}. It still exists as of today, in good condition except for a few mechanical issues. Currently running Legacy Showtapes. This store is notable for being featured in a 2011 video where a Chuck E walkaround's head fell off during a Cupid Shuffle live show.
