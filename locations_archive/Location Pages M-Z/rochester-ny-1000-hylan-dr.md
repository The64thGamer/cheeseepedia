Rochester, NY was a Chuck E Cheese location located at 1000 Hylan Drive.

## History

This location opened up in 2001, opening with Phase 3 and a Studio C Alpha stage. It remained that way up until 2012. In 2012, they would receive the 2012 update to the 2003 Cool Chuck Reconfiguration. This location sadly shut down on June 25, 2020, due to the bankruptcy.
