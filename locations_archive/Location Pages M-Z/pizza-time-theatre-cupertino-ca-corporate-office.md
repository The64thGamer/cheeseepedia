The ***Pizza Time Theatre Corporate Office* was the main office used for *Pizza Time Theatre Inc.* between 1978 to late January of 1979.<sup>(1)</sup>
The building was located on *10060 Bubb Rd. Cupertino, CA 95014.*<sup>(2)</sup>**
