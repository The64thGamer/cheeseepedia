***75 Consumers Dr* is an *Chuck E. Cheese's* that opened on October 12, 2001 with a {{< wiki-link "Studio C Alpha" >}}, and is still open to this day.**

## Stages

This location, as mentioned before, features a Studio C Alpha stage. It is still at the location as of May 2024. The {{< wiki-link "Bird (Animatronic)" >}}, {{< wiki-link "Pizza Phone (Animatronic)" >}}, and Apple TV would all be removed between 2022-2024. The desk was removed in 2023.

## History

When it opened in 2001, the Whitby location was a Phase 3 store. This changed in 2014. Whitby currently has the 2013 update to the {{< wiki-link "CEC 2000's Remodel Program" >}}, which includes a new game package, the exterior being updated from the 1998 Cool Chuck exterior to a Rockstar Chuck exterior, and also has Rockstar Chuck on the artwork.
