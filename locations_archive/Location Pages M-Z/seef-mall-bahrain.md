**Seef District, Manama, Bahrain, AlSeef Mall Manama Seef** is a Chuck E. Cheese that opened on October 30, 2021, with a Dance Floor, which is still open to this day. This location is the very first location in the country of Bahrain, and is franchised by the Masbi Group, which are also slated to open new Chuck E. Cheese locations in Kuwait and Oman in the future(1).
