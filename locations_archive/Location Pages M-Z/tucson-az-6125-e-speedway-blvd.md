***Tucson, AZ (6125 E Speedway Blvd)* is a *Chuck E. Cheese* that open in October 2014 with a {{< wiki-link "Circles of Light" >}}. In March 2023, the store received the 2.0 remodel. It is still open today with the 2.0 Remodel. This store was an relocation of the {{< wiki-link "Tucson, AZ (6130 E Speedway Blvd)" >}} location, which open in April 1995 with a {{< wiki-link "1-Stage" >}}, and closed in October 2014.
This is not to be confused with the {{< wiki-link "Tucson, AZ (5534 E 22nd St)" >}} Pizza Time Theatre that opened July 15th, 1980, and closed in 1987 with a Portrait Stage. This is also not to be confused with the {{< wiki-link "Tucson, AZ (8616 E Broadway Blvd)" >}} Pizza Time Theatre that opened on May 4th, 1981, and closed in 1983 with a Balcony Stage.**
