***Pasadena, CA (3765 E Foothill Blvd)*** was a *ShowBiz Pizza Place* which opened on December 15, 1982 with a {{< wiki-link "Classic Stage" >}}, and closed in October 1993, as a *Chuck E. Cheese's Pizza* with a {{< wiki-link "Concept Unification Stage" >}}. The store relocated to {{< wiki-link "Pasadena, CA (3737 E Foothill Blvd)" >}}, which is still open to this day. This location is known for being the last ShowBiz Pizza location to undergo {{< wiki-link "Concept Unification" >}}.
